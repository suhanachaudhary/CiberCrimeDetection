About Online Crime Reporting


Welcome to our Online Crime Reporting Portal, an initiative created to empower students to report and share information about cybercrimes within our college community. In today's digital world, the rise of online threats has become a significant concern, and it's essential to take proactive steps to ensure the safety of all users.


Our platform provides a safe, secure, and confidential space for students to report incidents related to cybercrimes, such as online harassment, identity theft, phishing, and more. We are dedicated to raising awareness about cyber safety and helping prevent the misuse of digital platforms.

This project is developed by students, for students, with the goal of fostering a supportive environment where everyone feels protected. By encouraging transparency and collaboration, we strive to create a safer digital experience for all.



Our Mission

To promote cyber safety, raise awareness, and empower students to report cybercrimes without fear, ensuring a safer online environment.



Our Vision

To build a proactive community that addresses and prevents cyber threats through information sharing and vigilance.


  • Empower Students: Foster a supportive environment where students feel confident and safe to report cybercrimes without fear of judgment or retaliation.
  • Promote Cyber Awareness: Educate and raise awareness about the latest cyber threats and how to prevent them, empowering our community to stay safe online.
  • Encourage Vigilance: Build a culture of proactive vigilance, where students and staff work together to identify and prevent cyber threats.
  • Create a Safe Digital Space: Strive to make the internet a safer place by reducing instances of cybercrime through information sharing and community engagement.
  • Confidential Reporting: Ensure that all reports are handled with the utmost confidentiality, encouraging more students to come forward and share information.
