Welcome To CampusGaurd

we’re here to make sure that every member of our campus community feels safe and supported. If you’ve experienced or witnessed any kind of crime or safety issue, we’re here to provide a secure and confidential platform where you can report it easily and, if preferred, anonymously.

Why Report?

Reporting incidents helps protect our community, ensures timely assistance, and promotes a culture of safety and respect. By speaking up, you’re contributing to a safer environment for everyone at Institute of Engineering & Technology Lucknow

How We Help

Confidential Reporting: Choose to stay anonymous if you prefer.

Quick Assistance: We work with campus authorities for immediate action on reported incidents.

Supportive Resources: Access information on counseling, legal advice, and other campus services.

Real-Time Updates: Stay informed on campus safety alerts and updates.

Safety Tips & Resources

Stay Aware of Your Surroundings Avoid distractions like headphones or phones when walking alone, especially at night. Be conscious of people and activities around you.

Use Well-Lit Paths Stick to well-lit, populated areas when moving around campus after dark. Avoid shortcuts through secluded areas.

Trust Your Instincts If something feels off, trust your gut. Leave the situation and head to a safe place or call for help.

Know Campus Security Contacts Save the campus security phone number in your contacts and keep it easily accessible for emergencies.

Travel in Groups When possible, walk with friends or classmates, especially at night. There’s safety in numbers.

Report Suspicious Activity Don’t hesitate to report any suspicious activity or behavior. If you see something, say something.

Counseling & Support Services If you’ve experienced a crime or are feeling unsafe, reach out to our counseling services for emotional support and guidance.

Campus Security & Escort Services Our campus security offers 24/7 patrols and, in many cases, escort services if you need assistance getting across campus.

Together, We Can Make a difference

At CampusGaurd , we believe in fostering a campus where everyone feels empowered and secure. Your voice matters—join us in making Institute of Engineering & Technology Lucknow a safer place.


Empowering Students to Report Cybercrime

Our platform empowers college students to report cybercrimes securely and anonymously. We aim to raise cyber awareness, provide a safe space for sharing information, and foster a safer digital community. Join us in the fight against cyber threats!
